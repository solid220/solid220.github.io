about

about

I've been working as a designer in the Washington metropolitan area for 19+ years. Currently I am a lead UI/UX designer at Enlighten an HII Company. With my background in graphic design, I help create "solid" visual concepts and aim to bring simplicity to complex digital products. My position requires close collaboration with end-users or product owners and the engineering team to ensure our efforts move in the same direction towards meeting users’ needs and goals.

Being a designer requires me to be creative, hence I am always on the lookout for inspiration, seeking fresh perspective. To this day, I am constantly learning on the job.

  • Graduated from University of Maryland (UMD) in 2002 with a BFA in graphic design.
  • Holds an active TS Security Clearance by DoD CAF
